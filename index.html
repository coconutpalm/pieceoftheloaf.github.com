A Piece of the Loaf

A small portion to bless God's family of faith

This site aims to contain:

  • A study notebook around what it means to be God's family, authored by a variety of people from different local gatherings.
  • Music and songbooks to support these sorts of gatherings

Everything here may be (re)distributed freely for any noncommercial purpose, so that it can be a gift to Jesus, and to his Bride. (Specific license terms are below, simply to make sure that all of the intellectual property distributed on this site is clean.)

Content and Downloads

Study Notebook

The study notebook is available in interactive HTML (one long page), HTML (one page per section), and PDF formats.

The Body of Christ

A video has been added that was originally for discussion among some of the brothers in our local assembly. But as feedback has been that people seem to find it useful, it is offered here for consideration and edification.

This web site is, by necessity, a work in progress. Content is added here frequently, and without notice. :)

Collaboration

As the whole loaf of Christ's Body is only together when the Assembly is together, the more people who contribute to the resources available here, the more complete this site will be.

As a result, to enable collaboration, free and open tools are used to produce 100% of this web site. If anyone else has produced Bible studies that harmonize with what is here or has written their own original worship songs that are used in your church, I would be glad to consider your work for inclusion here.

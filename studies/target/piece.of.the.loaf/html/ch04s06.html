4.6. Your prayer has been heard

Today, we live near the end of church history. The institutional Western church has aged and is no longer producing spiritual children able to transform our culture. Institutional church attendance in the decline and peoples' hearts are growing progressively colder.

But Jesus is coming. And the Lord has called to himself a people prepared for him.

We too have an impossible task: to bear these spiritual children. Spiritual children who, like John the Baptist:

  • Will be great in the sight of the Lord (as opposed to the sight of man)

  • Will not drink the wine of this world's pleasures

  • Will be filled with the Holy Spirit, even from (before) their spiritual birth

  • Will continue the task of turning many more of God's chosen people to the Lord their God

  • Will go as forerunner before the Lord...to make ready for the Lord a people prepared for him

Similar to Zechariah, we cannot do this task in our own ability.

Just like Zechariah, we can only pray that God would give us these children.

And like Zechariah, the Lord sends his angel to announce that our prayers are according to God's will. That these prayers are heard. And that as we are faithful, the Lord will provide, just as he said.

Do not be afraid, Zechariah, for your prayer has been heard.

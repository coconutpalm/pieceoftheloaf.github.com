Part I. When you come together

The Last Supper. The very mentioning of this term puts images in our hearts and minds that may or may not match what actually happened in Scripture. So, what exactly happened? Let's dive into Scripture and ask that question. What we find might surprise, amaze, or even offend. But in order to be faithful to God's revelation in his Word, we really must ask, must we not?

  • Chapter 1, “Do this in remembrance of me” — simply reconstructs in outline form exactly what happened that first night Jesus met with his disciples, and asks a crucial question: “If Jesus invited us to do this in remembrance of him and Paul adds that by doing this we proclaim our Lord's death until he comes, is there any good reason not to do just that?”.
